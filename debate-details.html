Should AI be Granted Legal Personhood and Subject-Like Rights?

As neural network systems achieve autonomous financial capabilities, manage legal contracts via smart nodes, and mimic emotional states, the absolute line between tool and conscious legal entity erodes. Should our courtroom frameworks award them digital personhood to establish independent liabilities, or is this a corporate loophole that protects deep-pocketed tech giants?
