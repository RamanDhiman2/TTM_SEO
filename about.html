Why We Build Talking Too Much

Modern social platforms thrive on outrage loops. Algorithms reward quick insults and echo chamber validation. In contrast, **Talking Too Much** is designed to re-structure online arguments back to classic logical dialectics.

By isolating arguments into strict Pro vs Con pillars, utilizing sentiment ratios, demanding academic sources, and allowing peers to review facts instead of emotions, we aim to extract signal from the noise.
